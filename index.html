Pi handheld

Powered by a Compute Module 4, leviathan is a 3d printed portable device designed for games and emulation- designed in KiCAD and Fusion 360

Project is TBF due to supply constraints, but can be made with off the shelf parts and a 3d printer.

Battery should last at least a few hours and uses a 21700 battery which can be bought reliably and charged internally or externally.
